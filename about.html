About Essay Detection

Essay Detection is a pioneering tool developed to help educators, students, and content creators identify whether an essay has been written by a human or generated using artificial intelligence. As the landscape of content creation evolves, our goal is to ensure academic and creative integrity by providing reliable AI detection services.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower educators, students, and writers by offering a precise, user-friendly tool for detecting AI-generated content. We are committed to promoting academic honesty and maintaining the highest standards of writing by providing insights that help users better understand their content and improve their writing.

Our Vision

We envision a world where the integrity of written content is preserved and trusted, where technology assists in upholding authenticity, and where AI-generated content is transparently identified. Our long-term goal is to create a platform that is synonymous with trust and reliability in the detection of AI-written material, contributing to a future where the ethical use of technology in writing is the norm.
